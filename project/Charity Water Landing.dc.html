A story of change · Tigray, Ethiopia

When the walk gets shorter, everything changes.

For years, women here began each morning the same way — a long walk to a distant pond to fill a jerry can with the same water their animals drank from. Hours of every day, gone to a chore that often left their families sick.

Then a clean water source arrived close to home. The walk got shorter. Girls who once carried water went back to their classrooms. Mothers found hours to farm and earn. Waterborne illness fell away. Clean water didn't just change a day — it changed what was possible.
